Chapter Two

How a story becomes a scarf.

Step 1 You share a story. It can be a place, a moment, a person. You tell us what matters most, and we listen. Really listen. The details that make it yours — the blue shutters, the fig tree, the bridge you crossed on your wedding day.

Step 2 We draw it by hand. Our artist, Shivantika, creates an original illustration that captures the feeling of your story. You'll see a preview before anything is printed. This is a collaboration, not a factory.

Step 3 Your keepsake arrives. Printed on pure silk, your scarf ships to your door. Ready to wear, to frame, to wrap around someone you love, or to hold onto for the next chapter.
